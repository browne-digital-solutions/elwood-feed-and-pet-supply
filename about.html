Our Story

Serving East Northport & The Surrounding Long Island Community

Elwood Feed Pet Supplies was created to give local customers a convenient, friendly place to find pet food, feed, farm supplies, lawn products, garden products, and everyday animal care essentials close to home.

Local Products. Friendly Help. Real Convenience.

Instead of dealing with crowded big-box stores or driving farther than necessary, customers can shop locally for the products they need most.

From dog food and cat litter to livestock feed, hay, straw, bedding, lawn supplies, and farm essentials, the store is designed to support a wide range of local customers.

With free local delivery available, Elwood Feed Pet Supplies makes it easier for families, pet owners, farms, and stables to stay stocked.

Community Focused

A Better Local Shopping Experience

Elwood Feed Pet Supplies focuses on the things that matter most to local customers: quality products, fair pricing, helpful service, and convenience.

Whether you are caring for a dog, cat, bird, small animal, chickens, horses, goats, sheep, cattle, or a property that needs regular supplies, the store is built to be a dependable local resource.
